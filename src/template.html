About me

I like to see myself as a Product Engineer rather than just a Frontend Developer. That's because I enjoy participating in almost all stages in the Product Development Lifecycle. From Ideation, to writing out Software Requirement Specification, creating high-fidelity Mockups (UI/UX Design), and turning those mockups into functional products with Code. My involvement allows my to contribute to key product decisions that will enhance the users experience.


I also enjoy collaborating with cross-functional teams such as the Design, Makerting, Product, and Data teams in order to achieve the product & company goals.


I've worked as a QA Engineer and Marketing Manager at notable Technology Startups across Southeast Asia & West Africa, such as KAODIM & ARIGA where I worked as a Software QA Engineer, and Marketing Manager respectively.


Due to my passion for spreading Digital Literacy amongst the African youth, I founded DIGILIT AFRICA amongst other startups.
